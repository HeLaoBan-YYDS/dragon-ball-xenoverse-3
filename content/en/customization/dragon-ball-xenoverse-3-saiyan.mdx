## The Saiyan Race in Age 1000: Lore and Background

## Next-Gen Customization and Soul Bonding for Saiyans

Customizing your **dragon ball xenoverse 3 saiyan** is no longer just about picking hairstyles and armor colors. Dimps is introducing a groundbreaking system called **Soul Bonding**. This mechanic is a direct evolutionary step, combining elements from previous titles like *Dragon Ball FighterZ* and *Dragon Ball: The Breakers*.

### How Soul Bonding Works
In previous games, players had to train under specific mentors to unlock their signature moves. In this third entry, the Z Warriors exist as souls. By forming a spiritual connection—or "Soul Bond"—with these legendary figures, your custom Saiyan can inherit their combat styles, passive traits, and ultimate attacks.

This system allows for unprecedented customization. For example, a **dragon ball xenoverse 3 saiyan** can bond with the soul of Goku to master the Kamehena or Kaioken, or bond with Vegeta to unleash a devastating Final Flash. The deeper your bond with a specific warrior, the more effectively you can execute their moves, offering a highly personalized progression path.

Community reports suggest that Soul Bonding will also alter the visual aura of your custom character. A Saiyan bonded deeply with Vegeta might exhibit a sharper, more jagged blue aura, while a Goku-bonded warrior will display a softer, traditional white-and-blue energy flare.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### What makes the Dragon Ball Xenoverse 3 Saiyan customization different from previous games?
The biggest difference lies in the introduction of the **Soul Bonding** mechanic and the jump to next-generation hardware. Instead of simply learning moves from mentors, your **dragon ball xenoverse 3 saiyan** will bond spiritually with the souls of iconic Z Warriors, inheriting unique passive traits, combat styles, and stat modifiers. Additionally, the visual fidelity of transformations, clothing physics, and character models has been completely modernized for PS5, Xbox Series X/S, and Steam.

### Can Saiyans have tails in Xenoverse 3, and do they animate?
Yes, according to early community reports and promotional artwork, Saiyan custom characters can be created with tails. Unlike previous entries where tails were static accessories, the next-gen game features fully animated tails that react dynamically to your character's movements, combat stances, and transformations.

### When is the release date for Dragon Ball Xenoverse 3?
The game is officially scheduled for a **2027** release in Japan and worldwide. It is being developed by Dimps and will be published by Bandai Namco for PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S, and PC (Steam).

### Will there be a school or academy system in the game?
Yes. The story mode reveals that Bulma and Brett enroll your custom character in a specialized school within New City. Here, you will learn the mechanics of the world, train with other recruits like Lilica, and take on special missions as part of the Great Saiyan Squad (GS Squad).
