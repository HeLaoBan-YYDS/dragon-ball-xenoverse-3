### Saiyans: Tail Mechanics and Great Ape Transformations
Saiyans remain the most popular choice among the player base. In early promotional trailers, community reports highlighted a male Saiyan character sporting a distinct 80s/90s retro hairstyle standing in an alleyway—complete with a fully functional, animated tail.

### Earthlings (Humans): Martial Arts Legacy and Tech-Infused Skills
Earthlings are set to receive a major lore update in Age 1000. Namekians are poised to benefit directly from recent anime developments. ### Frieza Race: Custom Multi-Form Progression

For the sequel, players are hoping for a system where starting body modifications dictate subsequent transformations. Imagine designing a hulking Second Form, a monstrous Third Form, and a sleek Final Form for a single character, culminating in Golden or even "Black" Frieza states.

In previous entries, male Majins were locked into a heavy-set build modeled after Majin Buu. Community reports suggest that Dimps is working on a more flexible customization path, allowing players to choose between a slim "Super Majin" aesthetic or a chaotic, kid-like variant.

By establishing a Soul Bond with these legendary heroes, your custom character can temporarily channel their power, traits, and ultimate attacks. Soul Bonding completely redefines how the **dragon ball xenoverse 3 playable races** interact with legendary fighters, bypassing the traditional master-student grind of previous games.

## FAQ

### What are the confirmed dragon ball xenoverse 3 playable races?
While Bandai Namco has not released the finalized roster, trailers and franchise history confirm the return of the core five races: Saiyans, Earthlings (Humans), Namekians, the Frieza Race, and Majins. Advanced technological themes in the trailers also point toward Androids and Demons as highly probable new additions.

### Will Saiyans finally have moving tails and Great Ape transformations?
Yes, early trailers showcase Saiyan characters with fully animated, physics-based tails. The presence of Great Ape imagery in promotional materials strongly suggests that Saiyans will finally have access to the giant Oozaru transformation, bringing them in line with the Namekians' Giant form.

### How does the new Soul Bonding mechanic work?
Soul Bonding allows your custom character to bond with the souls of legendary Z Warriors from past eras. This mechanic grants your CaC access to specific move pools, stat boosts, and passive traits, with certain races receiving unique synergy bonuses when bonding with historically compatible heroes.

### Will there be a playable Android race in Xenoverse 3?
While not officially confirmed as a starter option, the heavy focus on the Red Ribbon Army's legacy and the appearance of Gamma 1 in the promotional material strongly suggest that Androids will be playable. Community reports indicate they may feature passive Ki regeneration in exchange for the inability to manually charge energy.
