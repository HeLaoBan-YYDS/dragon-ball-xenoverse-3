## Saiyan Transformations: Beyond the Blue

It is no secret that Saiyans are the most popular race in the game. For **dragon ball xenoverse 3 transformations**, the challenge isn't finding new forms—it's deciding which ones to prioritize. With the introduction of Gohan Beast and the continuation of the manga, the ceiling for Saiyan power has reached astronomical heights.

### Super Saiyan 4 and the Primal Path
One of the most requested features is the inclusion of Super Saiyan 4 as an Awoken Skill rather than just a separate character slot (like GT Goku or Vegeta). Player experience suggests that the transformation should involve a physical change, including the iconic red fur and tail. This could be balanced by making it a "physical-heavy" form, boosting Strike Supers while offering less of a boost to Ki Blasts compared to the "God" forms.

### Ultra Instinct and Beast
While Ultra Instinct is currently available in Xenoverse 2, its implementation is often criticized for being too passive. For the sequel, fans want a version that feels more active. Similarly, "Beast" has set a new standard for power. These forms should represent the pinnacle of the Saiyan (and potentially Human) experience, requiring intense training and specific stat thresholds to unlock.

## Majins and Earthlings: Creative Potential

Majins and Earthlings often have the most creative gameplay loops, but their transformations have historically been lackluster. To make **dragon ball xenoverse 3 transformations** truly special, these races need forms that change how the game is played.

For Majins, the "Purification" form is often disliked because it replaces the player's custom moveset with Kid Buu's moves. A better approach for the sequel would be an "Absorption" mechanic. This would allow a Majin to temporarily absorb the traits of a defeated opponent or a mentor, granting them access to specific buffs or a "Fit Majin" state that increases agility.

Earthlings, on the other hand, could tap into the "Max Power" form used by Master Roshi. This would turn the lean Earthling avatar into a hulking powerhouse, focusing on raw strength and high-impact physical strikes. This would provide a nice contrast to the more "spiritual" or "technical" forms used by other races.

## Mechanics: Balancing Power and Stamina

A major concern with adding more powerful **dragon ball xenoverse 3 transformations** is game balance. If Ultra Instinct or Black Frieza are too strong, no one will use anything else. The solution lies in a more nuanced stamina and Ki management system.

1.  **Tiered Drain:** Higher-tier forms should drain Ki or Stamina faster, requiring the player to be efficient with their attacks.
2.  **Transformation Stages:** Instead of selecting "Super Saiyan 1" or "Super Saiyan 2" as separate skills, the game should allow players to power up through stages during a single match, similar to the [official Dragon Ball games](https://www.bandainamcoent.com/games/dragon-ball-xenoverse-2) of the past.
3.  **Risk vs. Reward:** Forms like "Ultra Ego" (if included) could increase the damage the player deals as they take damage, creating a high-risk playstyle.
