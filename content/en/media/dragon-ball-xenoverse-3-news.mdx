## Rebuilding the Foundation: A New Engine

Perhaps the most technical piece of **dragon ball xenoverse 3 news** involves the game's internal architecture. Executive Producer Masayuki Hirano has confirmed that Xenoverse 3 is not being built on the foundation of the first two games. While Xenoverse 1 and 2 shared a core engine, the third entry features a completely rebuilt engine designed for the current generation of hardware (PS5, Xbox Series X|S, and PC).

This decision is a double-edged sword for the community. On one hand, a new engine allows for vastly improved physics, more dynamic environments, and a level of visual fidelity that matches modern titles like *Dragon Ball Sparking! ZERO*. On the other hand, rebuilding from scratch means that the massive roster of over 100 characters from Xenoverse 2 cannot be "copy-pasted" into the new game. The development team has acknowledged that bringing every fan-favorite character forward will be a significant challenge, but the trade-off is a mechanical depth that the series has lacked until now.

## The "Action Performance" System and Customization

A unique detail emerging from recent interviews involves a focus on "style" and "attitude." Producer Hirano-san has expressed a personal fondness for the Ginyu Force, specifically because of how they express themselves through posing and unique animations. This has directly influenced a new mechanic in Xenoverse 3: the Action Performance system.

This system allows players to customize not just their movesets, but their "screen presence." In previous games, your character's personality was largely determined by their race and a few preset voice lines. In Xenoverse 3, players can explore and customize how their character poses, enters a fight, and expresses themselves during combat. This level of player expression is intended to foster a more social and "weird" community hub, reminiscent of the early days when players would spontaneously form Ginyu Force squads in the lobby.

### Joining the Great Saiya Squad

The narrative structure has also evolved. Instead of being a standard Time Patroller, players will find themselves guided into the ranks of the **Great Saiya Squad**. This faction serves as the primary defensive force in Age 1000. As part of this squad, you will rise through the ranks, take on missions, and interact with a new cast of original characters designed based on Akira Toriyama’s final notes and lore documents.

1.  **Enlistment:** Create your hero and choose your racial origin.
2.  **Training:** Master the basics of the new combat engine in West City.
3.  **Promotion:** Earn your place in the Great Saiya Squad by completing futuristic story arcs.
4.  **Specialization:** Customize your "Action Performance" to stand out in the online hub.

Instead, West City is designed to be a place where players actually want to spend time, whether they are posing with friends, trading gear, or participating in localized city events that trigger dynamically.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### When is the Dragon Ball Xenoverse 3 release date?
According to the latest official **dragon ball xenoverse 3 news**, the game is scheduled to release in 2027. It will be available on PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X|S, and PC.

### Will my progress from Xenoverse 2 carry over?
Because Xenoverse 3 is built on a completely new engine, a direct transfer of characters and items is unlikely. However, the developers have hinted at "veteran rewards" for players who have a history with the previous games, though the exact details have yet to be confirmed.

### What is "Age 1000" in Dragon Ball Xenoverse 3?
Age 1000 is a futuristic setting in the Dragon Ball timeline, occurring roughly 200 years after the end of the Z-Saga. It features a world where Earthlings have integrated Saiyan and Namekian traits, technology is highly advanced, and the "Great Saiya Squad" protects the peace.

### Is the game coming to Nintendo Switch or older consoles?
Currently, the **dragon ball xenoverse 3 news** only confirms the game for PS5, Xbox Series X|S, and PC. Due to the rebuilt engine and the focus on high-fidelity "action performance," there are no current plans for a release on PS4, Xbox One, or the original Nintendo Switch.
