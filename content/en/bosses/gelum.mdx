## Overview

Gelum is the first boss you encounter in VV: ULTIMATUM, located in the Fort Adams tutorial area. This fight is designed to teach you the core combat mechanics: parrying, dodging red attacks, and punishing recovery frames. You cannot fail the tutorial the goal is learning, not sweating.

- **Location:** Fort Adams (tutorial area)
- **Type:** Tutorial Boss Dark Souls-style knight
- **Recommended Level:** Tutorial (any level)

## Attack Patterns

Gelum has a straightforward but punishing moveset for new players:

- **Heavy Sword Swings:** Slow, telegraphed horizontal and vertical slashes. These are your primary parry windows.
- **Charge Attacks:** Gelum lunges forward with a powerful thrust. Watch for the wind-up animation and dodge to the side.

## Strategy

- **Learn parry timing:** Gelum's sword swings have clear wind-ups. Practice the parry window it is generous for a tutorial boss.
- **Dodge red attacks:** Red-glowing attacks cannot be parried and must be dodged. This mechanic carries through the entire game.
- **Stay close and punish recovery:** After each combo, Gelum has a brief recovery window. Stay in melee range and land hits during these openings.
