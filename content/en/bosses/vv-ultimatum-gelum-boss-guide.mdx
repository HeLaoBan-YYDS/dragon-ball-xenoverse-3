## Understanding Gelum's Core Mechanics: Movement is Key

The fundamental principle for defeating Gelum is **constant movement**. Unlike many boss encounters where blocking or relying on invincibility frames might save you, Gelum's primary threat comes from unblockable Area-of-Effect (AoE) attacks. These attacks are telegraphed by a distinct red aura appearing around Gelum or on the ground.

### Evading AoE Attacks

When you see a red aura, consider it an immediate evacuation notice. There's no blocking this damage, and invincibility frames (i-frames) from dodges won't consistently save you if you linger in the danger zone. Your only reliable defense is to **move out of the affected area immediately**. Even a split second too long can result in significant damage.

This emphasis on movement means you should never remain static for long. Gelum's fight is designed to keep you on your toes, constantly pressuring you with these AoE attacks. Utilize normal dodges for minor adjustments and, if available, abilities like "flash step" or other fast movement skills to quickly reposition yourself out of harm's way. Staying mobile ensures you're always ready to react to the next red zone.

## The Art of Offense: Critical Hits and Posture Breaks

While defense is paramount, you won't defeat Gelum by simply running away. Offense comes in bursts, specifically through the strategic use of your critical hit abilities.

### Leveraging Critical Hits

Critical hits are your main damage dealers in this fight. They are crucial for two reasons:
1.  **Breaking Gelum's Block:** Gelum can block attacks, but a well-timed critical hit can bypass this defense.
2.  **Lowering Posture:** Critical hits significantly reduce Gelum's posture bar. Once his posture is broken, he becomes vulnerable to a flurry of attacks, creating a valuable damage window.

Keep a close eye on your critical hit abilities' cooldowns. As soon as they are ready, don't hesitate. Close the distance and land those hits. This aggressive, yet calculated, approach with critical abilities is what truly speeds up the fight and prevents it from becoming a drawn-out war of attrition.

## Exploiting Gelum's Weakness: No Ranged Attacks

One significant advantage players have against Gelum is his lack of ranged attacks. This particular weakness offers a crucial safety net if the fight becomes overwhelming.

### Creating Distance to Reset

If you find yourself cornered, low on health, or simply need a moment to collect yourself, don't panic. Since Gelum has no ranged projectiles, you can safely create distance. Back away from the boss, reset your position, and allow yourself to regroup. This breathing room is invaluable for recharging abilities, assessing the arena, or simply regaining composure before re-engaging.

When Gelum inevitably starts chasing you, remember his primary method of closing the gap is still through his predictable AoE attacks. Continue your mobile strategy, dodge the red zones, and avoid getting cornered. This tactical retreat and re-engagement cycle is a powerful tool in your arsenal against the VV Ultimatum Gelum boss.

## Mastering the Flow: Combining Movement and Attacks

The true mastery of the Gelum fight lies in seamlessly integrating your defensive movements with your offensive windows. It's a rhythm of evasion and aggression.

### Reading Attack Patterns

Gelum's AoE attacks, while dangerous, are predictable. After a few encounters, you'll start to recognize the tells and timing of his red aura. This allows you to anticipate his moves and pre-position yourself for a safe dodge, rather than reacting at the last second. Player experience suggests that consistent observation of these patterns is far more effective than trying to memorize a rigid sequence.

### The Attack-Dodge Cycle

Your combat loop should look something like this:
1.  **Engage and Attack:** Get in close and land some normal hits while waiting for your critical abilities to come off cooldown.
2.  **Watch for Red:** As soon as you see a red aura, disengage immediately and move out of the AoE.
3.  **Reposition:** Use dodges or movement abilities to get to a safe spot.
4.  **Critical Strike:** When your critical ability is ready, close the distance again and unleash it to break his block or posture.
5.  **Repeat:** Continue this cycle, adapting to Gelum's movements.

This dynamic approach ensures you're dealing damage efficiently while minimizing your exposure to his devastating AoE attacks. Community reports highlight that players who adopt this rhythm report significantly faster and less frustrating victories against Gelum.
