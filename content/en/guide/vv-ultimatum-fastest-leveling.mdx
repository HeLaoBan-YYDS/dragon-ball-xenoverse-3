One of the most effective strategies for **VV Ultimatum fastest leveling** involves repeatedly tackling low-rank missions, particularly the medium-difficulty Menos mission. ### Acquiring Mission Tickets: Your Key to Endless XP

To continuously run low-rank missions like Menos, you'll need Mission Tickets. These tickets are essential for initiating missions and are a critical component of the **VV Ultimatum fastest leveling** strategy. While there's no guaranteed drop method, player experience indicates that killing random Hollows in the Soul Reaper area is the most reliable way to acquire them.

Although ticket drops are based on RNG, the drop rate is generally considered fair, not excessively low. The key is persistence: keep eliminating Hollows, and you will eventually gather enough tickets to fuel your mission farming. The more tickets you accumulate, the longer you can sustain your high-XP mission runs without interruption. Focus on farming these tickets diligently in the early game.
