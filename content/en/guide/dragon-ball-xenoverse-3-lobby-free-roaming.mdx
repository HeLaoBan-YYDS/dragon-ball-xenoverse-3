## Troubleshooting Connection Issues in the New Hub

A major hurdle for any online game is server stability. During the launch windows of previous titles, many players reported difficulties connecting to the multi-lobby. Player experience reports often mention that the "Online Lobby" option might appear greyed out or return an error message if the servers are under heavy load.

To ensure you have the best experience with **dragon ball xenoverse 3 lobby free roaming**, it is vital to check your NAT type. Gaming consoles and PCs often struggle with multiplayer connectivity if the NAT type is "Strict" or "Type 3." Switching to an "Open" or "Type 1/2" connection usually resolves most issues where other players appear to be "lagging" or disappearing from the lobby.

### Common Connectivity Solutions

*   **Check Server Status:** Always visit the official [Bandai Namco Support](https://www.bandainamcoent.com/games/dragon-ball-xenoverse-2) page to see if there is scheduled maintenance.
*   **Ethernet vs. Wi-Fi:** For a stable free-roaming experience, a wired connection is always superior to wireless.
*   **Regional Settings:** Ensure your game region matches your actual location to reduce latency when interacting with others in the city.
*   **Update the Game:** Developers frequently release "stability patches" in the weeks following a game's launch.
