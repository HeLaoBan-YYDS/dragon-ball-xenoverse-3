<Section icon={Target} title="Shinigami Progression: From Soul Reaper to Shikai">
The path to unlocking Shikai as a Shinigami is a multi-step process that combines questing, meditation, and combat. Each stage builds upon the last, ensuring players are adequately prepared for the challenges ahead.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Run Main Story Quests">
    Start by completing Main Story Quests across various areas like Fort Adam, Soul Society Outskirts, The Marsh, Arctic Plains, and Hueco Mundo. These quests provide essential experience and unlock later Soul Society tools. Use **Sense (C)** to locate NPCs if you get lost.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlock Soul Society and Buy the Soul Pager">
    After completing early main quests, press **U** to open the faction portal and travel to Soul Society. In the Hub, purchase a **Soul Pager** from Kou, the mission NPC. This tool is vital for requesting and joining missions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Farm Mission Tickets">
    Consistently farm Mission Tickets by finishing Main Story and Daily Quests. You can also get them from defeating NPCs (Hollows, Dragonflies, Bats) and opening chests. Running out of tickets will slow your progression significantly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reach Level 25 and Start Meditation">
    Grind quests and missions until you reach **Level 25**. Once at Level 25, press **L** to meditate. Look for a green aura that signals the start of Shikai progression.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Respond to Spirit Whispers">
    After your first meditation, **Spirit Whispers** will appear as one-line messages above your HP bar. Immediately press **L** to meditate when a Whisper appears to earn Shikai EXP. If meditation only puts your character to sleep, return to quests and missions to gain more XP.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Don't Ignore Whispers">
Ignoring Spirit Whispers will stall your Shikai progression. Always respond promptly to maximize your Shikai EXP gain.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="The Inner World: Bells Puzzle and Riddles">
Once you've made sufficient progress through meditation, you'll enter the Inner World to face the bells puzzle. This is where the "invasion bell" concept comes into play, as you interact with these white bells to proceed.

<Steps>
  <Step num="6" title="Solve the Bells Puzzle">
    Inside the white void of your Inner World, follow the blue orbs. Interact with the white bells you encounter. Each bell will either trigger an NPC fight or present a riddle. Solve all bells; completed bells will turn blue and remain saved, even if you fail a riddle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="7" title="Clear the Fog and Reach Level 35">
    After completing the bells puzzle, meditate again. If a fog-covered Inner World quest appears, return to the main world. You must reach **Level 35** and complete the required main story step before meditating back into the Inner World to clear the fog.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Riddle Solutions for the Inner World Bells

The bells will present riddles that you must solve to progress. Here are the known riddles and their answers:

| Question                                              | Correct Answer |
| :---------------------------------------------------- | :------------- |
| I cannot talk, but I always reply when spoken to. What am I? | **2 - An Echo**  |
| What gets broken without being held?                  | **1 - A Promise** |
| What is always in front of you, but can’t be seen?    | **1 - The Future** |

<Callout type="info" title="Riddle Tip">
Wrong riddle answers will send your character back to the main world, but any bells you've already solved will remain completed. You won't lose progress on solved bells.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Confronting the Shikai NPC and Unlocking Abilities">
The final stage of Shikai obtainment involves a challenging fight against your Inner World Shikai NPC, followed by unlocking your new abilities.

<Steps>
  <Step num="8" title="Fight the Shikai NPC">
    Find the Shikai NPC in the Inner World. You'll first complete an easier fight. After this, collect more Shikai EXP through meditation and return to start the full boss fight.
  </Step>
  <Step num="9" title="Unlock Shikai Abilities">
    After obtaining Shikai, meditate again. Talk to the Shikai NPC in the Inner World, enter the white void, and interact with the silhouette of your Shikai weapon. Use the **Ability tab** and **Ability Tokens** to manage or reroll your Shikai abilities.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Shikai NPC Boss Phases

The Shikai NPC fight is a multi-phase encounter that tests your combat skills. Understanding each phase is crucial for victory.

| Phase           | Description                                                                                                                                                                  | Key Strategy                                                                   |
| :-------------- | :--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Base Form**   | Uses the player's skills and passives, mirroring your own combat style.                                                                                                         | Learn its attack patterns, which are your own. Exploit openings.               |
| **Second Form** | Revives with unique moves in addition to the player's moves and passives.                                                                                                      | Be prepared for new abilities. Maintain distance if necessary, or pressure aggressively. |
| **Final Form**  | Regenerates **four HP bars** after dropping below three bars and switches weapon behavior, making it highly resilient.                                                            | Focus on burst damage when its HP is low. Adapt to new weapon movesets.         |

<Callout type="success" title="Victory Strategy">
The fight can be retried if you fail. The practical solution is to gain more XP, refine your timing, and ensure your build can withstand the specific matchup. The weapon unlock and ability unlock are separate steps, so complete both before considering your Shikai route finished.
</Callout>
