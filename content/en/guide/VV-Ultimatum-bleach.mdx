<Section icon={ScrollText} title="Shinigami Progression and Shikai Unlock Path">
The Shinigami path in VV Ultimatum involves a structured journey from a fresh Soul Reaper to mastering your Zanpakuto and unlocking Shikai. This route emphasizes questing, mission participation, and a unique meditation system to deepen your bond with your weapon. Early progression focuses on main story quests to build levels and unlock access to the Soul Society.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Run Main Story Quests">
    Complete Main Story Quests across areas like Fort Adam and Soul Society Outskirts to build early levels and unlock the Soul Reaper progression loop. Use Sense (C) to find NPCs and defeat open-world enemies for XP.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlock Soul Society and Buy Soul Pager">
    After initial quests, press U to open the faction portal and travel to Soul Society. Purchase a Soul Pager from Kou near the Hub to access repeatable missions, which are crucial for consistent progression.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Farm Mission Tickets">
    Mission Tickets are essential for running Soul Society missions. Obtain them from Main Story and Daily Quests, defeating NPCs like Hollows and Dragonflies, and opening chests. Running out of tickets will slow your progress.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reach Level 25 and Start Meditation">
    Grind quests and missions until you reach Level 25. Press L to meditate. Look for a green aura, indicating the start of Shikai progression. After this, Spirit Whispers become key triggers for Shikai EXP.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Respond to Spirit Whispers">
    Watch for one-line Spirit Whispers above your HP bar. Immediately press L to meditate when they appear to earn Shikai EXP. If meditation only puts you to sleep, return to quests until another Whisper appears.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Solve the Bells Puzzle">
    Upon entering the Inner World, follow blue orbs and interact with white bells. Each bell triggers an NPC fight or a riddle. Solve all riddles; completed bells turn blue and save your progress. Incorrect answers send you back to the main world, but solved bells remain.
  </Step>
  <Step num="7" title="Clear the Fog and Reach Level 35">
    Meditate again after completing the bells puzzle. If a fog-covered Inner World quest appears, return to the main world, reach Level 35, complete the required main story step, then meditate back into the Inner World.
  </Step>
  <Step num="8" title="Fight the Shikai NPC">
    Locate and fight the Shikai NPC in the Inner World. Complete the initial easier fight, then collect more Shikai EXP before returning for the full boss battle. After defeating and executing the boss, hold E to activate your Shikai weapon.
  </Step>
  <Step num="9" title="Unlock Shikai Abilities">
    Meditate again after obtaining Shikai. Talk to the Shikai NPC in the Inner World, interact with your Shikai weapon's silhouette in the white void, then use the Ability tab and Ability Tokens to manage or reroll Shikai abilities.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Shinigami Progression Warning">
Ignoring Spirit Whispers or running out of Mission Tickets can significantly delay your Shikai progression. Prioritize these elements for efficient advancement.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Hollow Progression: From Basic to Resurrección">
The Hollow progression in VV Ultimatum is a journey of predatory evolution, focusing on consuming other Hollows, accumulating traits, and eventually transforming into an Arrancar with access to Resurrección. This path rewards strategic trait selection and patience.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Choose a Hollow Type">
    Start your Hollow route by selecting one of four basic Hollow bodies: Gorilla (balanced), Lizard (speed, lower defense), Turtle (defense, lower speed), or Centipede (high speed, lower defense/damage). Your choice matters for your later Resurrección fight.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Consume Hollows and Collect Traits">
    Defeat and consume other Hollows to gain progression and traits. Accept traits that align with your final build (e.g., Iron Skin, Wings, Deadly Claws, Cero traits) and reject those that don't. Base Hollows hold 2 traits, Adjuchas 3, and Vasto Lorde 4.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Reach Menos">
    Continue consuming Hollows until you evolve into Menos. At this stage, decide whether to rush Arrancar or push for stronger Hollow forms like Adjuchas and Vasto Lorde. Rushing to Menoscar early limits you to two trait slots.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Push to Adjuchas">
    Complete in-game evolution requirements, such as destroying objects or defeating targets, to advance to Adjuchas. This form unlocks stronger Hollow routing, Cero access, and a third trait slot.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Aim for Vasto Lorde">
    Follow the main red questline and Hollow progression requirements to reach Vasto Lorde. This is the optimal Hollow foundation, allowing for four traits before your final evolution into an Arrancar.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Become Arrancar or Vastocar">
    Trigger your evolution once your chosen Hollow stage is ready. This shifts your progression from monster forms to weapon and Resurrección development. Select an Arrancar weapon route that complements your build.
  </Step>
  <Step num="7" title="Meditate for Resurrección">
    Equip your active weapon and meditate when inner-world whispers appear above your skill hotbar. These whisper windows are crucial for efficient Resurrección progress. If meditation only puts you to sleep, gain more XP.
  </Step>
  <Step num="8" title="Win the Resurrección Fight">
    Enter the Inner World through successful meditation and fight your original Hollow type. This fight can be retried if you fail, allowing you to improve your build or timing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success">
Successful Hollow progression hinges on thoughtful trait selection and patience. Don't rush mask removal; build your trait set strategically for a powerful Arrancar form.
</Callout>

</Section>
