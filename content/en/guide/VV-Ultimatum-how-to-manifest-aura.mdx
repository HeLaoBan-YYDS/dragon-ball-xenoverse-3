<Section icon={Activity} title="The Meditation Loop: How to Manifest Aura">

The most common way to manifest your aura is through the meditation system. This is a dedicated progression loop that begins at Level 25. Unlike other Roblox RPGs where aura is a simple toggle, here it is earned through consistent interaction with your spiritual self.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Level 25">
    Grind Main Story Quests in areas like Fort Adam or Soul Society Outskirts. You cannot trigger the spiritual awakening aura before this level.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Initial Meditation">
    Press **L** to begin meditating. If you are Level 25, you will notice a soft green aura surround your character. This confirms you have entered the progression state.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Monitor Spirit Whispers">
    While playing normally, watch the area above your HP bar. A line of text (Spirit Whisper) will occasionally appear. This is the "call" of your aura.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Respond and Gain EXP">
    Press **L** immediately when a whisper appears. This grants Spiritual EXP. As this EXP grows, your aura will become more distinct and intense during combat.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Important">
If you meditate without a Spirit Whisper active, your character will simply fall asleep. This does not provide progression toward manifesting a stronger aura or unlocking Shikai.
</Callout>

| Meditation State | Visual Indicator | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Base Meditation** | No particles | Health/Stamina recovery only |
| **Awakened State** | Green smoke/aura | Progression toward Shikai/Resurrección |
| **Inner World Entry** | White screen/Void | Solving puzzles (Bells) to solidify aura |
| **Combat Manifest** | Faction-specific glow | Active buff during Shikai or Blut usage |

<Section icon={Shield} title="Troubleshooting Aura Manifestation">

Many players struggle with **VV Ultimatum how to manifest aura** because the game does not explicitly explain the "visibility" stat. If you have completed your Shikai or Resurrección but cannot see your aura, check the following table for common fixes.

| Issue | Potential Cause | Solution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **No Green Aura** | Under Level 25 | Level up via Main Story Quests first |
| **Aura Disappears** | Low Reiatsu (Mana) | Charge your energy or wait for regen |
| **Can't see others** | Missing "Sense" skill | Unlock Sensory 1 in the Misc tree |
| **Whispers not appearing** | Meditation EXP capped | Solve the Bell Puzzle in the Inner World |
| **Aura is too faint** | Low Spiritual Potential | Invest more SP into the Misc tree nodes |

<Callout type="warning" title="Warning">
The "Aura" visual is significantly reduced if your graphics settings in Roblox are set to 1-3. For the full manifestation effect, ensure your graphics are at 5 or higher.
</Callout>
