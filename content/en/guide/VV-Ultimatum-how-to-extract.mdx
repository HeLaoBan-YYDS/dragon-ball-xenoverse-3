<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Extraction Process">

The extraction process in VV Ultimatum generally follows a predictable pattern, though specific objectives may vary. Here’s a general guide to ensure you successfully extract your hard-earned rewards.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate Extraction Point">
    Upon entering a zone, identify the available extraction points on your map. These are often marked with a distinct icon. Some zones may have multiple options, offering strategic flexibility.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Clear the Area">
    Before initiating extraction, ensure the immediate vicinity of the extraction point is clear of hostile NPCs and potential player threats. A clear zone reduces the risk of interruption.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initiate Extraction Sequence">
    Interact with the designated extraction beacon or objective. This will typically start a timed sequence, making you vulnerable. Be prepared for incoming threats.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Defend the Zone">
    During the extraction timer, you will likely face waves of enemies or aggressive players attempting to stop you. Utilize your skills and abilities to defend your position until the timer completes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Successful Extraction">
    Once the timer reaches zero, you will be transported out of the zone, securing all your collected loot and progression.
  </Step>
</Steps>
