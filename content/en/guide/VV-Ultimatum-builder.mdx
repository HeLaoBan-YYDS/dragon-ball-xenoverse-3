<Section icon={Shield} title="Progression Paths: Hollow, Shinigami, and Quincy">
Each faction in VV Ultimatum offers a unique progression journey, which is a key consideration for any **VV Ultimatum builder**. Understanding these routes helps you plan your long-term character development.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Hollow Progression: From Menos to Vasto Lorde">
    The Hollow route is about evolution and trait collection. Start by choosing a Hollow type (Gorilla, Lizard, Turtle, Centipede) and consume other Hollows to gain progression and collect traits. Aim for Vasto Lorde before becoming Arrancar to maximize your trait slots (up to 4). After becoming Arrancar, meditate to unlock and win the Resurrección fight, which is crucial for your final build.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Shinigami Progression: Shikai Unlock Path">
    Shinigami progression focuses on quests, missions, and mastering your Zanpakuto. Complete main story quests to unlock Soul Society and acquire a Soul Pager for missions. Reach Level 25 and begin meditation; respond to Spirit Whispers to gain Shikai EXP. Solve the Bells Puzzle in the Inner World, reach Level 35, and then defeat your Shikai NPC to obtain and unlock your Shikai abilities.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Quincy Progression: Reishi Archer Mastery">
    Quincy progression is built around mastering spiritual energy and ranged combat. While the game provides a clear starter weapon list for Quincy (Spirit Weapons like Sword, Spear), the core involves enhancing your Reishi manipulation and Kido scaling through specific skill trees. Focus on abilities like Heilig Pfeil and Blut techniques to control the battlefield from a distance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
Each progression path has unique milestones and challenges. For Hollows, trait planning is paramount; for Shinigami, it's the Shikai unlock; and for Quincy, it's mastering ranged pressure.
</Callout>
