No, according to the official site and promotional materials, the game is being developed specifically for the PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X|S, and PC (Steam). This allows the developers to utilize the advanced hardware of the current generation without being held back by older console limitations.

### Who is the new character Lilica mentioned in the trailer?
Lilica is a new character introduced in the "Welcome to West City" trailer. She appears to be a fellow student at the academy where the player enrolls. While her exact role in the story is still under wraps, she is expected to be a key ally in the GS Squad and the player's journey to becoming a champion of justice.
