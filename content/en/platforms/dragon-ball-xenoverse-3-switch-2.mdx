## Brand New Gameplay Mechanics Explored

The core combat has undergone a massive strategic overhaul. Rather than relying on simple button-mashing, fights now require careful resource management. Player reports highlight a new "Key Drain" system where specific physical attacks actively deplete your opponent's energy pool. Once their energy is completely gone, players can execute a "Break Smash" to trigger unique stagger animations.

Additionally, special moves now operate on individual cooldown timers. This forces players to cycle through their loadouts strategically instead of spamming a single powerful technique.

| New Mechanic | How It Works | Strategic Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Key Drain** | Specific attacks drain the opponent's energy directly. | Prevents defensive turtling and passive play. |
| **Break Smash** | Triggers a unique stagger when hitting an out-of-energy foe. | Opens up opportunities for massive ultimate combos. |
| **Move Cooldowns** | Special attacks require a recharge period after use. | Encourages diverse movesets and tactical variety. |
| **Soul Assist** | Allies briefly jump into the arena to perform a quick attack. | Offers defensive cover or helps extend active combos. |
| **Self-Revive** | Downed players can slowly recover if left undisturbed. | Rewards smart positioning and tactical retreats. |

The most revolutionary addition is the "Soul Change" mechanic. This allows your custom character to temporarily channel the spirit of a legendary fighter, completely altering your moveset, stats, and playstyle mid-battle. For instance, transforming into Future Trunks grants you instant access to his sword-based attacks and increased offensive power for a limited time.
