## Technical Overhaul: The Rebuilt Engine and Gameplay Mechanics

The decision to move **dragon ball xenoverse 3 pc** to a rebuilt engine is both a blessing and a hurdle for the developers. While it allows for significantly improved physics, lighting, and destruction, it also means that the massive roster of characters from Xenoverse 2 cannot be "copy-pasted" into the new game. Every character, move-set, and animation is being redesigned to fit the new mechanical framework.

Player experience reports from early previews suggest that the combat feels weightier and more deliberate. The "action performance" has been a major focus, with an emphasis on how characters express themselves during battle. This includes enhanced customization for poses and emotes—a feature the developers noted was inspired by the community's love for grouping up in hubs to perform Ginyu Force poses. The goal is to capture the "style and attitude" of Dragon Ball, not just the power levels.

### Redesigned Combat Mechanics
*   **Dynamic Environments:** More interactive stages that reflect the scale of high-powered battles.
*   **Customization Depth:** Expanded "action performance" options allowing players to fine-tune how their character moves and poses.
*   **Engine-Level Optimization:** Built specifically for SSD-based consoles and high-end PC hardware to eliminate loading stutters.
*   **Mechanical Refinement:** A ground-up redesign of ki management and stamina systems to encourage diverse playstyles.
