The primary argument for **dragon ball xenoverse 3 crossplay** is the longevity of the game. ### The Role of Rollback Netcode

One of the biggest hurdles for any fighting game is latency. Community reports and player experience discussions on forums often cite "lag switching" and "phantom hits" as the biggest issues in Xenoverse 2. For **dragon ball xenoverse 3 crossplay** to be successful, the developers must implement Rollback Netcode. This technology predicts player inputs to eliminate the feeling of delay, which is crucial when players on a PS5 in New York are fighting players on a PC in Tokyo.

### Cross-Progression: The Other Half of the Puzzle

Crossplay is often paired with cross-progression. Imagine starting your journey on your PC at home and continuing your character's leveling on a console in the living room. Given that Xenoverse is an RPG-lite experience requiring hundreds of hours of grinding for skills like "Power! A Lot of Power! It's Great!", the ability to move save data across platforms would be a massive win for the consumer.
