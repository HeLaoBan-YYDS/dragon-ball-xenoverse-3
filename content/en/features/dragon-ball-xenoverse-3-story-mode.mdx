## Setting the Stage: Welcome to Age 1000 and New City

One of the most significant changes in this upcoming sequel is its placement on the franchise timeline. The campaign takes place in Age 1000, which is exactly 148 years after the events of *Dragon Ball Xenoverse 2*. This chronological jump provides a completely blank canvas for the narrative, allowing the developers to explore a highly advanced Earth without being strictly tethered to the events of the original anime sagas.

At the heart of this new era is New City, a sprawling, futuristic metropolis that replaces Tokitoki City and Conton City as the central hub. Built with advanced Capsule Corporation technology, New City is a vibrant world where humans, Saiyans, Namekians, Majins, and Frieza Clan members coexist. To understand how the **dragon ball xenoverse 3 story mode** handles its timeline compared to its predecessors, consider the historical breakdown below.

### Table 1: Franchise Timeline Comparison

| Game Title | Primary Hub World | Core Timeline Era | Narrative Focus |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Dragon Ball Xenoverse** | Tokitoki City | Various (Time Patrol) | Correcting history anomalies in the DBZ timeline |
| **Dragon Ball Xenoverse 2** | Conton City | Various (Time Patrol) | Expanded history correction, including movie villains |
| **Dragon Ball Xenoverse 3** | New City | **Age 1000** (148 years later) | Original future storyline, school life, and local heroism |

Akira Toriyama's creative vision serves as the foundation for this futuristic setting. Before his passing, Toriyama laid out the conceptual framework for Age 1000 and personally illustrated multiple character designs, including the default male and female player characters, Bulma's updated look, and a new key character named Brett.

## The Core Plot: The Great Saiyan (GS) Squad and School Life

The campaign begins with a classic isekai hook. Your custom character is mysteriously summoned to New City from "a land far, far away" with no memory of how they arrived. Upon waking up in a futuristic apartment managed by robotic assistants, you are greeted by Bulma and a energetic young fighter named Brett.

This narrative setup represents a major departure for the **dragon ball xenoverse 3 story mode** compared to previous games. Instead of immediately joining the Time Patrol to fight multiversal threats, your character must first adapt to everyday life in Age 1000. To help you integrate into society and test your latent abilities, Bulma and her allies enroll you in a local academy.

### Table 2: Story Mode Campaign Structure

| Campaign Phase | Primary Objective | Key Locations | Main Allies |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Phase 1: Arrival** | Learn the basics of New City and discover your origin | Player Apartment, New City Streets | Bulma, Brett |
| **Phase 2: Academy Days** | Enroll in school, meet classmates, and unlock basic transformations | New City High School, Training Grounds | Lilica, Park |
| **Phase 3: GS Squad Initiation** | Join the Great Saiyan Squad and protect the city from local threats | GS Squad Headquarters | Brett, Gamma 1 |
| **Phase 4: The Age 1000 Crisis** | Uncover a conspiracy threatening the peace of the future | Classified Locations | Future Trunks, Z Warriors |

At the academy, you meet classmates like Lilica and a rival named Park. However, school life is quickly interrupted by local criminal activity and mysterious enemy factions. To combat these threats, you are invited to join the GS Squad (Great Saiyan Squad), a superhero organization inspired by the legacy of Great Saiyaman. Alongside Brett—who wears a specialized, visor-equipped superhero suit—you will fly across New City to stop villains, rescue citizens, and establish yourself as a champion of justice.

## Gameplay Mechanics: Soul Bonding and New Combat Systems

While the narrative of the campaign is a major selling point, Dimps is also introducing massive overhauls to the core gameplay. The combat loop within the **dragon ball xenoverse 3 story mode** introduces a revolutionary system called **Soul Bonding**.

Soul Bonding is a unique mechanic that draws inspiration from previous Bandai Namco titles like *Dragon Ball FighterZ* and *The Breakers*. In Age 1000, the physical Z Warriors of the past may no longer be around, but their spirits can be summoned and bonded to your custom character. By forming a deep spiritual connection with legendary fighters like Goku, Vegeta, Piccolo, or Gohan, your character can dynamically inherit their movesets, passive buffs, and ultimate abilities.

### Table 4: Soul Bonding vs. Traditional Mentorship

| Feature | Traditional Mentorship (Xenoverse 2) | Soul Bonding (Xenoverse 3) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Access Method** | Find mentors physically located around the hub world | Unlock spiritual bonds through story progression and challenges |
| **Skill Acquisition** | Complete specific training missions to learn individual moves | Dynamically share skills and passive stat boosts in real-time |
| **Combat Impact** | No direct gameplay changes outside of equipped skills | Allows temporary stat boosts and unique dual-ultimate attacks |
| **Racial Compatibility** | Identical across all races | Enhanced bonuses when bonding with warriors of the same race |

This system allows for unprecedented customization. For example, a Saiyan custom character can bond with Goku to easily master the Kaioken Kamehameha, while a Namekian character can bond with Piccolo to unlock specialized defensive maneuvers. These bonds are forged by completing character-specific side quests, participating in historic battle simulations, and using them frequently in combat.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### When is the release date for the game?
While an exact day has not been finalized, Bandai Namco has confirmed that the game will launch in **2027** for PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S, and PC via Steam.

### How does the dragon ball xenoverse 3 story mode differ from previous games?
Unlike the first two games, which focused on correcting historical anomalies throughout the classic *Dragon Ball Z* timeline, the **dragon ball xenoverse 3 story mode** takes place in the year Age 1000. It features an original story set in the futuristic New City, where your custom character enrolls in school, joins the GS Squad, and uncovers a brand-new threat to the future.

### What is the Soul Bonding mechanic in the campaign?
Soul Bonding is a new gameplay system that allows your custom character to spiritually connect with legendary Z Warriors. This mechanic grants your avatar access to their iconic movesets, passive stat boosts, and unique combat skills, combining elements of character customization from previous fighting games and survival titles.

### Will the dragon ball xenoverse 3 story mode feature co-op gameplay?
While the main campaign is a narrative-driven single-player experience focusing on your custom character, community reports indicate that certain GS Squad missions, raid battles, and training challenges can be played cooperatively with friends online.
